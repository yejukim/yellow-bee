How It Works

From inquiry
to opening day.

Our franchise process is straightforward and designed to set you up for success from the very first conversation.

01
Week 1–2

Submit Your Inquiry

Fill out the form below with your background, desired market, and goals. We review every submission personally and respond within 5 business days.

02
Week 2–4

Discovery Call & Qualification

We schedule a call to learn about your background, financial position, and vision. This is a two-way conversation — you're evaluating us too.

03
Month 2–3

FDD Review & Agreement

Qualified candidates receive our Franchise Disclosure Document. Once you've reviewed with your attorney and are ready, we move to franchise agreement signing.

04
Month 3–6

Site Selection & Training

We work together to find your location, finalize build-out, and put you and your team through our training program at the flagship store.

05
Month 6–12

Grand Opening & Beyond

We support your launch with marketing assets, a pre-opening checklist, and on-site support for your first week of operations. Then we grow together.
